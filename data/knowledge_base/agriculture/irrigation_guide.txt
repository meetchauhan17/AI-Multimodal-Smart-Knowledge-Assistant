Domain: Agriculture
Document: Smart Irrigation Systems
Subject: Water Conservation in Farming

Irrigation Methods:
1. Drip Irrigation: Delivers water directly to plant roots via a network of pipes and emitters. Highly efficient; minimizes evaporation and weed growth.
2. Sprinkler Irrigation: Distributes water through overhead nozzles, mimicking rainfall. Best for lawns, pastures, and some row crops.
3. Subsurface Irrigation: Places drip lines below the soil surface. Minimizes surface evaporation and soil runoff.

Best Practices:
- Soil Moisture Monitoring: Use sensors to only irrigate when soil moisture drops below threshold levels.
- Timing: Water in the early morning or late evening to reduce evaporative water loss.
