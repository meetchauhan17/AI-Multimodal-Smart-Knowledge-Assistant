Domain: Library
Document: Central Library Code of Conduct
Audience: All Visitors and Members

Rules and Regulations:
1. Quiet Zones: Silence or whisper-level conversation only in designated reading rooms.
2. Mobile Devices: Set all phones to silent. Calls must be taken outside the library main hall.
3. Borrowing Limit: Registered members may borrow up to 5 books at a time for a duration of 14 days.
4. Late Fees: A fine of $0.50 per day will be charged for overdue books.
5. Care of Materials: Writing in, highlighting, or damaging library materials is strictly prohibited.
